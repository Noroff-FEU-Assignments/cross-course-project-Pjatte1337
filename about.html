About SQUARE EYES

What are we?

A video streaming website where you can purchase high-quality videos directly from the people who make them.

What we want?

At Square Eyes, we want to entertain the world. Whatever your taste, and no matter where you live, we give you access to best-in-class TV shows, movies and documentaries. Our members control what they want to watch, when they want it, with no ads, in one simple subscription. We’re streaming in more than 30 languages and 190 countries, because great stories can come from anywhere and be loved everywhere, and we’re always looking to help you find your next favorite story.

Content watchers: Supporting small filmmakers and discovering interesting films.
